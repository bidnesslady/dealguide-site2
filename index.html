Frequently Asked Questions

What is DealGuide AI?

DealGuide AI is a conversational assistant that helps you close more sales by guiding leads, answering questions, and overcoming objections in your voice and tone.

Who is this for?

It’s designed for coaches, digital product sellers, and real estate agents who want help converting curious visitors into paying clients — without doing all the selling themselves.

How does it work?

You train the AI by answering a few questions and providing your key selling points. We then create a tailored digital agent that can chat, follow up, and guide users to book or buy.

Can I customize my assistant?

Yes! You can choose how your assistant sounds, what it says, and where it appears on your site or store.

Do I need to know how to code?

No coding needed. If you can copy and paste, you can install and use DealGuide AI.
